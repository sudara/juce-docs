Rijmen, \char`\"{}\+The Whirlpool hashing function\char`\"{} NESSIE submission, 2000 (tweaked version, 2001), \href{https://www.cosic.esat.kuleuven.ac.be/nessie/workshop/submissions/whirlpool.zip}{\texttt{ https\+://www.\+cosic.\+esat.\+kuleuven.\+ac.\+be/nessie/workshop/submissions/whirlpool.\+zip}}
