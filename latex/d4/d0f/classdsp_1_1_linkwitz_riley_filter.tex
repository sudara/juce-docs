\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
\subsubsection*{template$<$typename Sample\+Type$>$\newline
class dsp\+::\+Linkwitz\+Riley\+Filter$<$ Sample\+Type $>$}
A filter class designed to perform multi-\/band separation using the TPT (Topology-\/\+Preserving Transform) structure.

Linkwitz-\/\+Riley filters are widely used in audio crossovers that have two outputs, a low-\/pass and a high-\/pass, such that their sum is equivalent to an all-\/pass filter with a flat magnitude frequency response. The Linkwitz-\/\+Riley filters available in this class are designed to have a -\/24 d\+B/octave slope (LR 4th order).
