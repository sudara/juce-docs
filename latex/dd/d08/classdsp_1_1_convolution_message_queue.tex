\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Used by the \mbox{\hyperlink{classdsp_1_1_convolution}{Convolution}} to dispatch engine-\/update messages on a background thread.

May be shared between multiple \mbox{\hyperlink{classdsp_1_1_convolution}{Convolution}} instances.
