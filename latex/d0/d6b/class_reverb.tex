\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Performs a simple reverb effect on a stream of audio data.

This is a simple stereo reverb, based on the technique and tunings used in Free\+Verb. Use \mbox{\hyperlink{class_reverb_a98c91d03e4a2484123dc3d8f92896b86}{set\+Sample\+Rate()}} to prepare it, and then call \mbox{\hyperlink{class_reverb_aba79aa61c18e8b3cf70dfd83fda7d099}{process\+Stereo()}} or \mbox{\hyperlink{class_reverb_a04c43dc7b91788df75f434a957499e2d}{process\+Mono()}} to apply the reverb to your audio data.
