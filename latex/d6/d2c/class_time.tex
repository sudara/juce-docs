{\footnotesize\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{class_string}{String}} Time\+::formatted (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{\mbox{\hyperlink{zconf_8in_8h_a2c212835823e3c54a8ab6d95c652660e}{const}} \mbox{\hyperlink{class_string}{String}} \&}]{format }\end{DoxyParamCaption}) const}

Converts this date/time to a string with a user-\/defined format.

This uses the C strftime() function to format this time as a string. To save you looking it up, these are the escape codes that strftime uses (other codes might work on some platforms and not others, but these are the common ones)\+:


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item a is replaced by the locale\textquotesingle{}s abbreviated weekday name.
\item A is replaced by the locale\textquotesingle{}s full weekday name.
\item b is replaced by the locale\textquotesingle{}s abbreviated month name.
\item B is replaced by the locale\textquotesingle{}s full month name.
\item c is replaced by the locale\textquotesingle{}s appropriate date and time representation.
\item d is replaced by the day of the month as a decimal number \mbox{[}01,31\mbox{]}.
\item H is replaced by the hour (24-\/hour clock) as a decimal number \mbox{[}00,23\mbox{]}.
\item I is replaced by the hour (12-\/hour clock) as a decimal number \mbox{[}01,12\mbox{]}.
\item j is replaced by the day of the year as a decimal number \mbox{[}001,366\mbox{]}.
\item m is replaced by the month as a decimal number \mbox{[}01,12\mbox{]}.
\item M is replaced by the minute as a decimal number \mbox{[}00,59\mbox{]}.
\item p is replaced by the locale\textquotesingle{}s equivalent of either a.\+m. or p.\+m.
\item S is replaced by the second as a decimal number \mbox{[}00,60\mbox{]}.
\item U is replaced by the week number of the year (Sunday as the first day of the week) as a decimal number \mbox{[}00,53\mbox{]}.
\item w is replaced by the weekday as a decimal number \mbox{[}0,6\mbox{]}, with 0 representing Sunday.
\item W is replaced by the week number of the year (Monday as the first day of the week) as a decimal number \mbox{[}00,53\mbox{]}. All days in a new year preceding the first Monday are considered to be in week 0.
\item x is replaced by the locale\textquotesingle{}s appropriate date representation.
\item X is replaced by the locale\textquotesingle{}s appropriate time representation.
\item y is replaced by the year without century as a decimal number \mbox{[}00,99\mbox{]}.
\item Y is replaced by the year with century as a decimal number.
\item Z is replaced by the timezone name or abbreviation, or by no bytes if no timezone information exists.
\item \%\% is replaced by \%.
