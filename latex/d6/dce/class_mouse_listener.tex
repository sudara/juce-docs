Called when the mouse first enters a component.

If the mouse button isn\textquotesingle{}t pressed and the mouse moves into a component, this will be called to let the component react to this.

When the mouse button is pressed and held down while being moved in or out of a component, no mouse\+Enter or mouse\+Exit callbacks are made -\/ only mouse\+Drag messages are sent to the component that the mouse was originally clicked on, until the button is released.
