\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This struct represents a playing MPE note.

A note is identified by a unique ID, or alternatively, by a MIDI channel and an initial note. It is characterised by five dimensions of continuous expressive control. Their current values are represented as \mbox{\hyperlink{class_m_p_e_value}{MPEValue}} objects.
