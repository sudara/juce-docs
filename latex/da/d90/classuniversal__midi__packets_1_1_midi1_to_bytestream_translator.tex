Converts from a Universal MIDI \mbox{\hyperlink{classuniversal__midi__packets_1_1_packet}{Packet}} to MIDI 1 bytestream format.

This is only capable of converting a single Universal MIDI \mbox{\hyperlink{classuniversal__midi__packets_1_1_packet}{Packet}} to an equivalent bytestream MIDI message. This function cannot understand multi-\/packet messages, like \mbox{\hyperlink{structuniversal__midi__packets_1_1_sys_ex7}{Sys\+Ex7}} messages.

To convert multi-\/packet messages, use {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{classuniversal__midi__packets_1_1_midi1_to_bytestream_translator}{Midi1\+To\+Bytestream\+Translator}}} to convert from a UMP MIDI 1.\+0 stream, or {\ttfamily \mbox{\hyperlink{classuniversal__midi__packets_1_1_to_bytestream_dispatcher}{To\+Bytestream\+Dispatcher}}} to convert from both MIDI 2.\+0 and MIDI 1.\+0. \mbox{\Hypertarget{classuniversal__midi__packets_1_1_midi1_to_bytestream_translator_a5f362659e25a4653f695a3394b7b512d}\label{classuniversal__midi__packets_1_1_midi1_to_bytestream_translator_a5f362659e25a4653f695a3394b7b512d}}
