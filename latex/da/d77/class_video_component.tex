\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A component that can play a movie.

Use the \mbox{\hyperlink{class_video_component_aa596daa7938bab6dad5ae25dd130fba9}{load()}} method to open a video once you\textquotesingle{}ve added this component to a parent (or put it on the desktop).
