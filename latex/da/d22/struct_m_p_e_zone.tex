\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This struct represents an MPE zone.

It can either be a lower or an upper zone, where\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item A lower zone encompasses master channel 1 and an arbitrary number of ascending MIDI channels, increasing from channel 2.
\item An upper zone encompasses master channel 16 and an arbitrary number of descending MIDI channels, decreasing from channel 15.
\end{DoxyItemize}

It also defines a pitchbend range (in semitones) to be applied for per-\/note pitchbends and master pitchbends, respectively.
